Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

AQS News ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AQS News mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Our App is a news aggregation application. We do not require user registration or login to use the App. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) from our users.

Log Data

We do not collect log data or usage analytics from the App.

Cookies

Our App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

Our App may display news content sourced from publicly available information. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

The App may store user preferences (such as display settings) locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. This data is not transmitted to any external servers and remains solely on your device.

Network Access

The App requires internet access to retrieve news content. The only network requests made by the App are to fetch publicly available news data. No personal information is transmitted during these requests.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk to user data. We are committed to ensuring that your experience with our App is safe and secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
